For those of you who dont know, I am Luna. Yes you see this name was granted to my humble self when i was brought to this world.

One may wonder, how did such a fair wee child like yourself turn out to be the beholded CEO of Swag, well buckle up true believers! For I shall tell the tale. You see when I was a wee guppy, my parents baked hot cross buns in the town square. They were humble bakers, but their goods were all but humble. You see, their pastries were artisinal. So one day the fair Lord of the land arrived to our town by chance. And Ye asked for a dozen of ye Hot Cross buns. This would be a simple feat among daily circumstances, but as fate would have it, only I was around to man the stand. I was a wee 2 foot shrimp with glass bones and paper skin. But with all my might i made those buns hot and crossed and bunny! And the King laughed at my attemps. But when his teeth sank into my bun, he fell to his knees. Tears streamed down their face. The clouds parted and bathed the land in glorious golden sheen. The birds rounded in awe and the air itself quivered. Fish did backflips and stuff too it was nuts. My Pastry was so delicious, and yummy. The king endorned me in all the finest furs and jewels, i bathed in pearls and satin. At last I was the CEO of Swag.
